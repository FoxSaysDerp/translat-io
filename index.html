How do we process our commissions?

1

After submitting your document via our form, we contact you immediately to discuss the project - its size, main points, what to keep in mind and price. When both parties agree on certain terms, we begin to start process your text.

2

Our translators are pointing out main issues of translating a text - title, chapters, acronyms, abbreviations etc. While having heads up for potential misconceptions and language barriers, we hard to keep original spirit of your creation. This is most time-consuming step, since depending on the size of text it takes from few day to several weeks.

3

When first version is ready, we send you the translation, indicating which aspects you should consider and for your review in general. We had experienced first version being final ones, but we absolutely acknowledge that is a rare case - after your discerning revision and direct feedback, we continue working on next iteration.

4

3rd step is actually going on and on until you are no longer concerned with quality of your new translation. After you feel content with our work, we polish the outcome and send it to you, with all required resources. Then we can succesfully conclude the project with you receiving your service and us receiving the payment.
